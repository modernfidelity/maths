# Mathematical Formulae and Identities 

## Pure Mathematics

### Quadratic Equations

$ax^2 + bx + c = 0 $ 

has roots

$\Large\frac{-b\pm\sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$

### Laws of Indices 

$a^x \times a^y \equiv a^{x+y}$

$a^x \div a^y \equiv a^{x-y}$

$(a^x)^y \equiv a^{x \times y}$


### Laws of Logarithms 

$x = a^n  \Leftrightarrow n = log_ax$ for $a>0$  and $x>0$

$log_ax + log_ay \equiv log_a(xy)$

$log_ax - log_ay \equiv log_a(\frac{x}{y})$

$k log_ax \equiv log_a(x^k)$
