## Laws of Indices 

1. $a^m$ x $a^n$ = $a^{m+n}$ 
2. $a^m \div a^n$ = $a^{m-n}$ 
3. $(a^m)^n$ = $a^{m \times n}$
4. $a^0 = 1$
5. $a^{-n} = \frac{1}{a^n}$
6. $a^{\frac{m}{n}} = (\sqrt[n]a)^m = (\sqrt[n]{a^m}) $
